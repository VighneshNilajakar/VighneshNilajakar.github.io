<h3 id="introduction">Introduction</h3>
<p align= "left" style="font-size:20px; padding: 10px;">Scrcpy, Abberivated from "Screen Copy" is an application that provides Mirroring and Controlling of an Android Device via USB or over A Network. It is a Multi-Platform, Open Source, and Free Application.
Your Android needs to be at least Android 5.0, also you need to enable USB or ADB debugging on your Android. Some Users might need to enable additional settings to control Mouse and Keyboard.</p>
